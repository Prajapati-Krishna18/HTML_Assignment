Exploring The Capital Of India :"JAIPUR"

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famously known as the “Pink City” for its distinct pink-colored buildings and royal heritage. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, the city is rich in history and Rajput architecture.

Jaipur is home to magnificent forts and palaces like the Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal, attracting tourists from around the world. The city's vibrant bazaars, selling handicrafts, jewelry, and textiles, add to its cultural charm.

Blending tradition with modernity, Jaipur also offers contemporary amenities, educational institutions, and growing infrastructure. It is a key part of India’s Golden Triangle tourist circuit, celebrated for its hospitality, colorful festivals, and historic grandeur.
